Tractor Machine Pile Foundation

We execute bored cast in-situ concrete piles with diameters ranging from 300mm to 750mm, drilled up to required depth or hard strata as per soil condition and structural design.

The scope of work includes boring, flushing, reinforcement fabrication and lowering, followed by concrete pouring using tremie pipe to ensure proper pile integrity.

Suitable for apartments, villas, commercial buildings, factories warehouses, STP and WTP plants.
